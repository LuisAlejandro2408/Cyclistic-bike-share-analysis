# Cyclistic Bike-Share Analysis

Exploratory data analysis of 5.7M+ bike trips to understand behavioral 
differences between casual riders and annual members, and provide 
data-driven recommendations to increase membership conversions.

---

## Key Findings

- **Casual riders take 58% longer trips** on average (19 min vs 12 min)
- **Members follow a commuter pattern** — bimodal peaks at 8AM and 5PM
- **Casuals peak on weekends**, members dominate weekdays
- **Casual ridership drops 93%** from summer peak to winter trough
- **Casuals outnumber members between 12AM–4AM** — an untapped segment

---

## Recommendations

| # | Recommendation | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Launch membership campaigns in spring | April — May |
| 2 | Target weekends with leisure-focused messaging | Saturday peak |
| 3 | Create late-night rider incentives | 12AM — 4AM |
